About The Fest

In the monotonous life cycle of a student, which mainly includes eating, sleeping, lectures, and repeating, ‘College Fests’ shows certain degree of fluctuations in the excitement levels of students. Why wouldn’t it? As the festive season slowly marches closer and closer with time, every student can taste the sweet scent of hard work; hard work of not only the students organizing it but the ones killing themselves with incessant practices to win that victory title.

Though, the only reasons why we should love fests are not because it acts as a catalyst to spark things up in one’s monotonous college schedule, but because of the additional perks. Yes! These exciting fests do a lot more to a student’s life than we know.Despite what people may say, winning an event however spectacular or ridiculous gives everyone something to boost about to the world. A shiny piece of metal awarded can skyrocket one’s self- esteem. Having the sweet realization of being accepted with your amazingly weird talents makes people feel important.

Face Painting


a) Maximum numbers of members in a team is 3.

b) Maximum numbers of colors that can be used while painting cannot be more than 6.

c) Time allotted for painting face cannot exceed 01:00 hour.

d) The painting will be judged on the following parameters:-
       1) Selection of Design.
       2) Neatness of the painting.
       3) Color combinations used.
       4) Time taken to finish the painting.
       e) Time penalty will be imposed if the process exceeds the        time limit allotted.
       f) The decisions taken by the judges will be final and abiding        and no complaints will be entertained in this regard.

Dance

a) The number of participants in the competition is of your choice.

b) It is the responsibility of the contestant/s to bring the required costumes needed for their performance. The organizers will not bear any responsibility.

c) The performance must not exceed 4 minutes.

d) The performance will be judged on the following parameters:-
       1) Dance.
       2) Costumes.
       3) Selection of Song/s.
       4) Performance time.

e) The judges themselves are renowned dancers and their decision will be final.


Duo cricket

c) Further rules will be informed during game play.


d)In case of Tie, we consider boundaries(4’s)

Crime investigation

a)Team size 4-6


b)Clues are presented at the crime scene.


c)Find the clues catch the culprit.

Food Without Fire

a) No use of fire is permitted in making the dish but ready made ingredients bought from market is allowed such as chips, biscuits.

b) The dish must be prepared during the competition and already prepared dishes are not allowed.

c) Each team will be given only one hour and 30 minutes for preparing their dish and if more time is taken, then a penalty will be imposed.
d) The performance of each team will be judged based on the following parameters:-
        1) Taste of the dish.
        2) Presentation of the dish.
        3) Cleanliness of the area allotted.
        4) Abiding to the time limit given.
        5) The food will be tasted by renowned chefs and their decisions will be final.

Singing

a) Maximum number of singers allowed is 7.

b) Musical instruments can be provided to those interested in participating in the contest.

c) The performance must not take more than 6 minutes otherwise a time penalty will be imposed.

d) The decision of the judges will be final and abiding and no complaints will be entertained in this regard.

e) Performances will be judged based on the following parameters:-

        1) The quality of singing.
        2) Selection of Songs.
        3) Time taken by the performance.
        4) Extra points if it appeals the audiences.
        f) If the participating colleges are bringing their own band then they must inform us about it before the 25th of February.
